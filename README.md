# The Team Principal

Welcome to **The Team Principal** — a groundbreaking motorsport management simulation where you step into the shoes of a racing team principal and guide your team to glory.  

This project is all about **depth, realism, and emergent gameplay**. Every decision you make—hiring drivers, developing cars, planning race strategy—matters. And thanks to our innovative **Cognitive Racing Framework (CRF)**, every driver on the track behaves as a unique, autonomous agent. Mistakes aren’t scripted—they happen naturally, as a result of real physics responding to each driver’s perception and choices.  

---

## 🎯 Vision

Our goal is simple: to create **the definitive motorsport management experience**. If you’ve ever wanted to run a racing team like you run a business, feel the tension of race weekends, or experience the thrill of strategic decision-making, this is the game for you.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

- **Cognitive Racing Framework (CRF):** Every driver acts autonomously based on perception, intent, and skill.  
- **Emergent Outcomes:** Race results are never scripted; everything unfolds naturally from your team’s decisions.  
- **Team Management:** Hire drivers and staff, develop cars, manage budgets, and navigate sponsor relationships.  
- **Race Strategy:** Plan every aspect of a race weekend, from qualifying to final lap, and experience the tension of real-time decisions.  
- **Season Progression:** Compete across a full championship, tracking standings, stats, and rivalries.
